Effects

Cyberbullying could cause victims to suffer emotional distress, anxiety, and depression.

It leads to a decline in self-esteem, academic struggles, and even physical health issues.

The impact extends to strained relationships, behavioral changes, and, in severe cases, thoughts of suicide. Beyond personal tolls, it tarnishes reputations and instills a lasting fear of technology. Addressing this issue is crucial through education, awareness, and preventative measures to safeguard mental well-being in the digital realm.
